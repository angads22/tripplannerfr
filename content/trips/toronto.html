Good to know
Up Yonge
Eaton to Souper is one straight walk north, about 15 min end to end. Mid-day needs zero transit — just keep heading up the street.
The PATH
From the waterfront, take the PATH (underground walkway) up to Eaton — skips weather, lights, and traffic.
Back to Union
Souper sits by College station. Subway one line down to Union, ~12 min. Don't walk it with the bus clock running.
Food density
Gyukatsu at noon plus hot pot at ~4:30 is two heavy meals fast, with dessert between. Want hot pot to feel like real dinner? Push it later and take the 8:26 bus, or skip Gyukatsu.
The one pin
Gyukatsu opens noon, lunch ends 3p. It's the only stop that can't drift — build the morning around it. Everything else flexes.
Karaoke = late bus
SU Karaoke doesn't open till 6p. Add it and you're committing to the 8:26 bus, not the 7:56.
Budget
Most stops are cheap or free (claw machines, browsing, dessert). The two real spends are lunch and hot pot — Souper keeps the hot pot affordable vs. Haidilao.
